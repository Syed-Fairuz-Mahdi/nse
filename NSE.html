CATS-MIST (NSE)




CATS-MIST (NSE) started in 2018 to provide material testing. The associated laboratories are the scanning electron microscopy (SEM) lab, the Wavelength Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (WDXRF) lab, and the High-Purity Germanium detector (HPGe) lab. Under the direct involvement and supervision of highly qualified professionals, CATS-MIST (NSE) has successfully conducted several testing works. CATS-MIST (NSE) is running with the best possible integrity and quality.

Currently, we are providing the following services:

  • Scanning Electron Microscopy (Imaging and Elemental Analysis)
  • Elemental Analysis by XRF
  • Elemental Analysis by WDXRF
  • Water Permeability Test

Nuclear Engineering Club




‘Nuclear for Peace’ with this motto MIST Nuclear Engineering Club started its journey in 2020 under the supervision of the department of Nuclear Science & Engineering, MIST. Our mission is to highlight the peaceful application of nuclear energy to all mindsets. We believe that the better solution will come if the problems are perceived deeply. We provide technical platform to the students to think and feel the problems of Nuclear Science & Engineering to innovate a healthier nuclear future. Undoubtedly, we inspire the students to explore the field of knowledge of Nuclear Science & Engineering by organizing many thrilling competitions. Our activities are not only offline, we are also active on an online platform to accomplish our ongoing and far-reaching mission.



Purposes

• To encourage the students about Nuclear Energy.

• To spread useful application of nuclear energy with online and offline publications.

• To organize innovative nuclear events beyond institutional boundaries.
